Activities in Paris!

What can you do in paris?

So you are wondering what you can do in Paris huh? Don't worry I got your back! Heres a list of this you can do in Paris

  1. Go punting
  2. Pony rides
  3. Canal cruising
  4. Belle-Epoque cycle
  5. Pétanque at Nation
  6. Climbing in Buttes Chaumont
  7. Paris plages
  8. Joséphine Baker swimming pool & solarium
  9. Best thing to do in Paris in my opinion is to EAT!

How much does it cost?

Activity Cost
punting €11 per hour
Pony rides €2.5 per ride
Canal cruising €16 per ride
Belle-Epoque cycle €22 per person (rental)
Pétanque at Nation Free!
Climbing in Buttes Chaumont Free
Paris plages Free
Joséphine Baker swimming pool & solarium €3
Eating €0-200
